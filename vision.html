Vision

Fostering an engaging multicultural community in Northeast Philadelphia for families, businesses, and neighborhoods and helping non-English speaking immigrants live whole, healthy lives with joy, safety, and dignity. We envision that with time, they will work alongside other Americans to build a strong society. They will bring prosperity to Philadelphia and their existence will be valued and appreciated.
