About Us

Northeast Philadelphia Chinese Association (NEPCA) is a grassroots community-based non-profit organization under the fiscal sponsorship of Culture Trust of Greater Philadelphia, a 501(c)(3) organization.

NEPCA assists the underserved non-English speaking Chinese immigrants in Northeast Philadelphia gain public services and benefits. It makes connections and builds partnerships with other organizations to provide services in the Chinese language. It provides public information in Chinese and organizes community activities to enrich lives, bridge cultural gaps, increase mutual understanding, advocates common concerns, and engage residents in community building.
